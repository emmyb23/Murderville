Welcome to Murderville USA

What is murder? Murder is defined by Merriam-Webster as the crime of unlawfully killing a person especially with malice aforethought.

Just how common is it? Our team of data scientists have combed through thousands of rows of data to bring you the most interactive and comprehseive visualizations for murders in the United States of America to solve all of your dying questions. We focused on extracting records from a murder database created by the FBI complete from all 50 states from years 2000-2014, all with great detail. We also gathered population data from Census.gov to get the most accurate counts of murders per 100,000 by location, as well as education and income levels down to the county. Combined, you will be able to formulate you own conclusions.... Will you be next?
